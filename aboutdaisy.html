About Daisy Li

Dedicated to her field, Daisy Li prides herself on prime negotiation, client-forward service, and ensuring the best result from an investment. A buyer’s agent, she has been called sincere and hardworking by her clients who value her work ethic, quick response times, comprehensive knowledge, trustworthiness, and honesty.

Working in real estate since 2015 provides Daisy with over six years of specialized experience. Between perfectly-staged homes and a deep understanding of how to earn the best possible gains, she’s created immense returns for the communities she works within,– Irvine, Newport Beach, and Lake Forest. She specializes specifically in getting the highest return available on investments. Recently, she has made the highest selling record per square foot for each listing in the neighborhood, demonstrating her unmistakable expertise.

Valuing the community in the neighborhoods where she works, Daisy is passionate about volunteering at the local food bank, especially since COVID-19, in addition to aiding seniors with grocery shopping and other at-risk, necessary activities. Daisy loves to spend out-of-office hours with her husband, daughter, and son and pursuing her other passions, including piano, hiking, interior design, and portrait photography.

After earning a bachelor’s degree in Education and then a master’s degree in International Journalism while living in London, Daisy worked as a program producer in a national TV channel and then as the founder of an outstanding design and marketing company. This long-term experience in leadership and commercial marketing roles has poised her to provide her clients with the best possible experience when it comes to the sometimes challenging process of selling a property. This is especially prominent when considering the importance of ingenuity of art, design, staging, and creativity in marketing, all skills Daisy brings to the table.

In addition to serving her communities, Daisy is also the principal broker in California of Seashine Property Service LLC, an affiliate of Seashine Capital Group. Seashine is a provider of REAL, the real estate lease acquisition program in 10 major states. In April 2021, Atalaya Capital Management announced to join Seashine to invest up to 250 Million dollars of its REAL Program assets.
